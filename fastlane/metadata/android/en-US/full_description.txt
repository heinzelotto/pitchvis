PitchVis listens to your microphone and turns the musical pitches it hears into a
colorful, real-time visualization.

Under the hood it runs a Variable-Q Transform (VQT) over the live audio signal, which
gives high frequency resolution in the low registers where music lives, then detects the
prominent pitches and maps each one to a color. The result is an animated picture of the
harmony, melody and tuning of whatever you play or sing.

Features:

* Real-time pitch analysis of live microphone input
* Variable-Q Transform tuned for musical signals
* Pitch-to-color mapping in a perceptual color space
* Several visual modes (Full, Zen, Performance, Galaxy)
* Tuning drift display in cents
* Runs fully offline — no network access, no tracking, no ads

PitchVis is useful for musicians, singers, music students and anyone curious about the
structure of the sounds around them. It works with any instrument or voice.

The app requires only the microphone permission and does not connect to the internet.
